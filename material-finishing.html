Material Finishing

Captivate Passersby: Street-side Allure

Our expertise lies in transforming base materials into the expected finished products that demand attention. Employing a range of surface treatments, our experts craft the envisioned results, adding that final touch of allure to your displays.

Refinement through Lacquering

Lacquering is our artistry, offering a spectrum of sheen levels, from an ultra-matte elegance to a radiant high-gloss finish, even incorporating antique colors to enhance material surfaces. This process ensures a smooth and resilient surface, elevating sturdiness while maintaining a refined aesthetic.

Employing an advanced system, our dedicated teams ensure uniformity and precision in every lacquering application, adhering to stringent quality standards. The result? When your visual stories reach their final stage, expect nothing short of the pinnacle of quality. Each item is crafted to the highest standards, poised to make a lasting impact and stand as a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Enhanced Protection with Powder Coating

Powder coating stands as a safeguard, fortifying surfaces of steel, plastic, glass, and MDF against scratches. This process creates a protective layer, offering a seamless shield of high-density resilience against corrosion, UV damage, abrasions, chemicals, and detergents.

One of its noteworthy advantages lies in its efficiency: with no air-drying or flash-off time required, it emerges as a time-effective method. Not only does this expedite the Visual Merchandising production process, but it also minimizes environmental impact. With powder coating, durability meets efficiency, ensuring surfaces remain resilient while contributing to a more sustainable approach to production.
